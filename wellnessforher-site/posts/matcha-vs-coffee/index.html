Matcha vs Coffee?? What's better for someone with PCOS?

Coffee and matcha can both fit into a wellness routine, but the best choice depends on how your body responds. Some people feel focused and energized with coffee, while others notice jitters, anxiety, or a blood sugar crash later in the day.

Matcha contains caffeine too, but many people describe the energy as steadier because it also contains L-theanine. Pairing either drink with food, hydration, and minerals can make a big difference in how you feel.

If you have PCOS, track your symptoms, sleep, cravings, and energy when you try each option. Your lived experience matters, and your healthcare provider can help you personalize the choice.
